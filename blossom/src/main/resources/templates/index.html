How it works!

It's pretty simple, just follow along.

01

Create a Parcel

The first step is to create a parcel of land. A parcel consists of "Sensors + Plant" You can create multiple parcels with multiple plants!

02

Add your Sensors

A good soil requires monitoring, you can add your Ph and Humidity Sensors!

03

Get a recomended Plant

If you still don't know what to plant blossom gives you recomendations based on the pH levels of your soil! Else skip to the next step

04

Add yourPlant

Select the plant which you are going to plant

05

Start Monitoring

This is the easy part, just sit back and blossom will give you any warnings if your plants are in danger and tell you when you need to water them
